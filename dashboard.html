About Cambridge EM School

Established in 2015, Cambridge EM School is a private unaided co-educational school in rural Manakondur block, Karimnagar district, Telangana. The school serves Grades 1-8 with English as the medium of instruction, and the academic session begins in April.

  • 14 instructional classrooms and separate headmaster room
  • Playground and library with around 400 books
  • Functional sanitation: 2 boys toilets and 3 girls toilets
  • Pucca boundary wall, all-weather road access, and electricity connection
